What is Ajira Digital?

Ajira Digital is a government initiative by Kenya's Ministry of ICT, aimed at empowering youth by providing free digital skills training and access to online job opportunities.

"But Ajira is not just about earning, it's about self-development, building confidence, gaining experience, and growing personally and professionally. Ajira gave me more than income – it gave me confidence, purpose, and hope."

Through Ajira, I received training in:

✓ Digital marketing
✓ Content writing
✓ Graphic design
✓ Transcription
✓ Free digital skills training
✓ Consistency and self reliance

The program offers:

✓ Free digital skills training
✓ Access to Ajira Youth Empowerment Centres
✓ Links to real and online jobs both locally and internationally

But Ajira is not just about earning money.

✓ Building discipline, consistency, and self-reliance
✓ Forming lifelong friendships and networks
✓ Gaining confidence to face the digital world with purpose.
